2. Fixing the Gameplay

Most stealth games are super frustrating. You get caught by a guard, you die, and you have to restart the whole level. It sucks. Urnique Studio used Design Thinking to fix this.

INDUSTRY PAIN POINT

"Game Over Fatigue" In traditional stealth games, making one mistake forces you to restart the level. This wastes time and makes players angry (Rage Quit).

URNIQUE SOLUTION

"The Scrub Bar" They turned the game into a media player. Made a mistake? Just rewind 5 seconds. This turned "Failure" into "Experimentation."
